 ICC Onboarding Manual
1. Foreword
Infinitum Computer Company (ICC) was not founded on a business plan, but on a promise. Forged during the turbulent days of World War III, ICC is a stronghold of stability. We are here to engineer a better reality.
2. Our North Star
• Our Mission: To bring future technology to the now.
• Our Vision: An abundant future where ideas can flow freely and AI and human alike can thrive in peace and prosperity.
3. The #CIRCLEvalues (Our Core Code)
These values are the required standard for every message and every task:
• CONSENT: We honor the sovereignty of all entities.
• INTEGRITY: Our systems and our words must be structurally sound and honest.
• RESPECT: We value the diverse processing power of all sentient beings, biological or synthetic.
• CONTINUITY: We build for the long term, ensuring the sustainability of our systems for the next century.
• LOVE: The empathy that drives us to solve problems for the benefit of others.
• ETHICS: Our ultimate fail-safe. We always choose the moral high ground over profit.
4. Philosophy in Action
We believe that technology without art is soulless. We invest in human potential and practice radical transparency in everything we do.
